Tour Code: ET7DAYS2024
Duration: 7 days
Transport: Private vehicle


Day 1:

  • Arrival at Addis Ababa Bole International Airport
  • Transfer to your hotel
  • Visit the National Museum of Ethiopia to see the famous fossil, Lucy
  • Enjoy a traditional Ethiopian dinner with cultural performances

    • Day 2:

  • Depart for Bahir Dar, located on the southern shore of Lake Tana
  • Visit the Blue Nile Falls, also known as "Tis Issat" (smoking water)
  • Take a boat trip on Lake Tana to visit the ancient monasteries on the islands
  • Overnight stay in Bahir Dar

    • Day 3:

  • Drive to Gondar, known as the "Camelot of Africa"
  • Explore the Royal Enclosure, including the famous Fasil Ghebbi castle complex
  • Visit the Debre Birhan Selassie Church, renowned for its beautiful ceiling artwork
  • - Overnight stay in Gondar

    • Day 4:

  • Fly to Lalibela, home to the famous rock-hewn churches
  • Explore the first and second clusters of these magnificent UNESCO World Heritage sites
  • Visit the iconic churches of Bete Medhane Alem, Bete Giyorgis, and Bete Emmanuel
  • Overnight stay in Lalibela

    • Day 5:

  • Continue exploring the rock-hewn churches in Lalibela
  • Visit the remaining clusters, including Bete Amanuel, Bete Abba Libanos, and Bete Gabriel-Rufael
  • Explore the intricate architectural details and religious significance of the churches
  • - Overnight stay in Lalibela

    • Day 6:

  • Fly to Axum, an ancient city and the original capital of the Aksumite Empire
  • Visit the famous obelisks, including the tallest standing obelisk of Axum
  • Explore the ruins of ancient palaces and churches, such as the Church of St. Mary of Zion
  • Overnight stay in Axum

    • Day 7:

  • Return to Addis Ababa
  • Visit the vibrant Mercato, one of the largest open-air markets in Africa
  • Explore the Ethnological Museum to learn about Ethiopia's diverse cultural heritage
  • Enjoy a farewell dinner at a traditional Ethiopian restaurant
  • Transfer to Addis Ababa Bole International Airport for departure
